SafetyGo

Safety is our priority.
SafetyGo will ensure your child knows when it's safe to go.

Transportation Modes

By School Bus

If you permit you child to ride the school bus, you will be able to track both the stops and times the bus your child is assigned to. We will make sure your child's homeroom teacher notifies you that your child has entered their assigned bus.

By Car

If you or an approved trusted guardian plans to pick up your child using a personal vehicle, you can register the license plate(s), which ensures the child's homeroom teacher that you have picked up your child.

By Foot or Bike

This option is only available if your child lives within a 10 mile radius of the school. If you permit your child to go home by foot or by their bike/scooter, we will make sure your child's homeroom teacher notifies you that your child has left.

Announcements From Toyota Elementary School

Toyota Elementary School's number one focus is safety. Schools dismissals often involve hundreds of buses, vehicles, and students walking or biking away from campus, which can pose hazards to drivers and walkers.
